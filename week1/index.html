UX/UI
Mobile First approach, even in Adobe xD?

Forms

  • Remove as many elements as possible.
  • No scrolling (split form if necessary).
  • Allow autocomplete.
  • Show password and add an option to hide.
  • Auto-advance to next field, especially for Credit cards
  • Use numberpad when necessary
  • Don't force copy paste for phone numbers or URLs

Questions

Having the menu on the bottom of the screen on mobile seems counter intuitive. It looks like it is the footer and there is nothing below it to scroll. Is it really a good idea? How long ago was the video on the menu made? Do people today still struggle with the hamburger menu? Would it depend on demographics as well?
